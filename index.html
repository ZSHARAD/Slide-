❓ Viva Questions & Answers

1️⃣ What is AI Project Cycle?

A structured 6-step approach: Problem Definition → Data Collection → Data Exploration → AI Modeling → Prediction & Evaluation → Continuous Improvement. It's a cyclical process that repeats for better results.

2️⃣ Why is data important?

Quality data is the foundation of accurate AI models. Good data leads to reliable predictions, meaningful insights, and helps us understand real patterns. Garbage in = Garbage out! More data + better quality = Better predictions.

3️⃣ What is modelling?

Creating a mathematical/machine learning algorithm that learns patterns from historical data to make predictions on new data. We use algorithms like Neural Networks, XGBoost, and Decision Trees to identify what factors cause student stress.

4️⃣ Why evaluate the model?

Evaluation ensures our AI is accurate, fair, and reliable before deployment. It helps us identify errors, measure performance, collect user feedback, and make continuous improvements for better real-world results.

5️⃣ How can AI reduce student stress?

Through early detection of stress patterns, personalized recommendations for better habits, automated support via apps, timely interventions by counselors, and continuous monitoring. AI provides data-driven insights for preventive care.
